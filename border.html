MANIFESTO / US-CANADA BORDER

Do not panic, remain calm.

Do not physically engage your apprehender

Do not let them separate you and your children at all costs.

Tell them that, according to the 2050 Canada-US Convention, you have the right of mobility.

Do not show them any and all forms of identification (IDs, papers, documents, etc).

Do tell them that you are travelling with Nation Without Borders. We have international and migration lawyers.

Do prevent encounters by travelling in large numbers. Stay with the caravan at all times.
