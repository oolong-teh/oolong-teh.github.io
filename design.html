MANIFESTO / SPECULATIVE DESIGN

  1. Design is intention and purpose.
  2. Design should be used for the betterment of society.
  3. Design was previously a vehicle for capitalism and fascism.
  4. Design was used to circulate erroneous stories and falsified information being passed off as facts.
  5. Design should now act outside the confines of the tyrranical administration.
  6. Design should be used to prepare for the future.
  7. Design cannot change the world alone.
  8. But, design can reach the people who can make these changes.
  9. Design can make these changes itself.
  10. Design is about empathy. Empathy instigates change.
